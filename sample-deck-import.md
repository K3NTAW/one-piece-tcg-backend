## How to Import This Deck

1. **Copy the entire deck list** (from the `******` markers)
2. **Go to Deck Builder** (`/deck-builder`)
3. **Click "Import Deck"**
4. **Paste the deck list** into the import dialog
5. **Click "Import Deck"** to load it into the builder
6. **Review and save** the deck to your collection

## Deck Strategy

This deck focuses on:
- **Aggressive early game** with low-cost characters
- **Character synergy** between Straw Hat crew members
- **Event cards** for removal and board control
- **Leader ability** to support the crew

## Card Breakdown

### Leader (1)
- **Monkey D. Luffy** - Provides support for Straw Hat characters

### Characters (38)
- **Roronoa Zoro** - Strong attacker with high power
- **Nami** - Card draw and utility
- **Usopp** - Ranged attacks and disruption
- **Sanji** - Life gain and protection
- **Tony Tony Chopper** - Healing and support
- **Nico Robin** - Knowledge and strategy
- **Franky** - Defense and technology
- **Brook** - Music and soul powers
- **Jinbe** - Fishman abilities and strength
- **Monkey D. Luffy** - Additional copies for consistency

### Events (12)
- **Gum-Gum Pistol** - Direct damage and removal
- **Three Thousand Worlds** - Zoro's signature attack
- **Straw Hat Crew** - Team synergy and buffs
